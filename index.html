Evan AI Assistant

Evan AI Assistant is an AI receptionist and scheduling automation app for businesses.

What this app does

Evan AI Assistant helps businesses answer calls, capture customer information, schedule appointments, and automate receptionist workflows.

How Google services are used

This app uses Google Calendar to create and manage appointments and Google Sheets to store and organize scheduling and customer workflow data.

Why access is requested

Google Calendar and Google Sheets access is requested only to provide scheduling, data organization, and workflow automation features for the user.

We do not sell user data or share user data with third parties except as required to operate the requested app functionality.
