Evan AI Assistant

Evan AI Assistant is an internal business reporting and workflow automation app.

What this app does

This app helps businesses collect, organize, summarize, and manage internal operational data. It supports reporting workflows by turning business updates into structured records, summaries, and reports.

How Google services are used

This app uses Google Sheets to store and organize reporting data, Google Docs to create and update reports, and Google Drive to access files created by the app or selected by the user for workflow purposes.

Why access is requested

Access to Google services is requested only to support internal reporting and workflow automation features. The app uses only the permissions needed to read, create, edit, and organize reporting data and documents authorized by the user.

We do not sell user data or use Google user data for advertising.
