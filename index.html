AI Receptionist Automation

This application provides an AI-powered receptionist that helps businesses answer calls, manage customer interactions, and automate appointment scheduling.

What the app does

The app integrates with Google services such as Google Calendar and Google Sheets to schedule appointments, organize business information, and support workflow automation.

Why Google data is requested

This application requests access to Google Sheets and Google Calendar only to enable scheduling and data storage features for the user.
