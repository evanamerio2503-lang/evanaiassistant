Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 16, 2026

This application is used for internal business reporting and workflow automation. It helps users collect, organize, summarize, and manage business data for reporting purposes.

Information We May Process

  • Business update data submitted by the user or team
  • Report content, summaries, notes, and workflow-generated outputs
  • Google Sheets data used for storing and organizing report information
  • Google Docs content used to create, edit, and maintain reports
  • Google Drive files that are created by the app or selected by the user for reporting workflows

How We Use Information

This application uses data only to provide the requested internal reporting and automation features, including:

  • Recording and organizing operational or project updates
  • Generating internal summaries and reports
  • Creating and updating Google Docs reports
  • Reading from and writing to Google Sheets for workflow automation
  • Accessing user-approved Google Drive files when needed for report creation or storage

Google User Data

If the user connects Google services, access is used only for the internal reporting features they authorize. Google Sheets data is used to store and organize reporting information, Google Docs is used to create and edit reports, and Google Drive file access is limited to files created by the app or files the user chooses to use with the workflow.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share user data with third parties for advertising purposes. Data is used only to operate the reporting and workflow automation requested by the user.

Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as reasonably necessary to operate the workflow, maintain reports, and meet applicable business or legal requirements.

User Controls

Users may revoke access to connected Google services at any time through their Google Account settings.
